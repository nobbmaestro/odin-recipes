Wifey's Neapolitan Pizza

No introduction needed! The Neapilitan Pizza is a Classic and will impress anyone who may be served. A perfect dish to be served on date or a party.

Ingrediances

A dinner for two!

For the Neapolitan Pizza Dough

Flour, 500 gram
Water (room temperature), 330 gram
Salt (fine), 12-15 gram
Yeast (dried of fresh), 0.2-0.5 gram
Cornmeal (for baking out the dough)

For the toppings

Tomato Sauce, 2 deciliter
Chévre Cheese, 180 gram
Prosciutto, 40 gram
Fresch Ruccola, 40 gram
Truffle Burrata, 2 pieces
Pesto Sauce

Method

The following steps are divided into two major activities, namely, preparation of the noapolitan pizza dough and baking of the pizza. The preparation of the noapolitan pizza dough shall be performed 1-3 days prior to the baking.

Preparing the Neapolitan Pizza Dough

  1. Mix the yeast and the water in a bowl. Make sure there are no lumps of yeast left.
  2. Add the salt and the floar and knead the dough.
  3. Leave the dough in the room temperature for 3-4 hours.
  4. Devide the dough into two equal halfs.
  5. Store the two dough halfs in an oiled in food box in a fridge for 24-72 hours.
  6. Store in the room temperature for 3 hours prior to the baking.

Baking the pizza

  1. Preheat the pizza stone on a barbeque, preferably to 300℃.
  2. Bake out the doughs by using the cornmeal to avoid sticky dough.
  3. Distribute the tomate sauce evenly and then add the chévre cheese.
  4. Bake the pizza on the preheated pizza stone on the barbeque for roughly 4 minutes.

How to serve

Apply the Prosciutto and roccula on top of the pizza and finish the topping by applying the pesto at will. Finally, place the truffle burrata in the middle of the pizza.

Preferably, serve the pizza with a glas of white wine.

Bon Appétit!
