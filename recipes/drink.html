Elderflower Mojito

A perfect thirst quencher for a hot summer day!

Ingrediances

  • Suger, 2 teaspoons
  • Fresh Lime Juice, 60 mililiter
  • Mint, 12 leaves
  • Elderflower Liqueur, 30 mililiter
  • Rum, 60 mililiter
  • Club Soda

Method

  1. Pour the sugar, lime juice and mint leaves into a glas and muddle the ingrediances to release the flawors.
  2. Add some ice and fill the glas with elderflower liqueur and rum.
  3. Top of with the club soda and stir the drink.

Serving

Preferably, garnish the glas with a lime wedge and serve on hot summer days.
