Chef's Favorite Burger

Ingrediances

  • Brioche Bun
  • Ground Chunk, 200 gram
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Caramelized Red Onion
  • Bacon
  • Truffle Mayonnaise

Procedure

  1. Preparing the caramelized union
    1. Slice the unions
    2. Preheat the butter in a pan
    3. Fry the unions
    4. Add one spoon of sugar and 2 spoons of water
    5. Stir until the water has evaporated

  2. Preparing the ground chunk
    1. Preheat the barbeque
    2. Press the 200 gram into a burger
    3. Season the meat by adding some flake salt and pepper on both sides
    4. Place the meat and bacon on the grill
    5. When the bacon is done, flipp the burger, add the cheddar cheese and place the bacon on the burger
    6. Roast the brioche buns slightly and move the buns and the meat on place

Apply one spoon of truffle mayonnaise onto the botten bun half, add some salad and apply the meat with the already applied cheddar cheese and bacon. Finally, place the caramelized unions and the top bun half. Preferably, serve the burger with sweet potate fryes.
