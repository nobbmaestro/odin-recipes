Chef's Favorite Burger

Summary tbd.

Ingrediances

A dinner for two!

For the Chef's Favorite Burger

  • Brioche bun, 2 pieces
  • Ground Chuck, 400 gram
  • Cheddar Cheese, 4 slices
  • Bacon, 140 gram
  • Truffle Mayonnaise, 2-4 spoons

For the Caramelized Red Onions

  • Red union, 1 piece
  • Sugar, 5 gram
  • Water, 50 gram or 0,5 deciliter

Method

The following steps are divided into two major activities, namely, preparation of the caramelized unions and preparation of the burger. These activities may be done simultaneously by one or two persons or even sequentially by one person. Whatever floats your boat!

Preparing the caramelized union

  1. Slice the unions
  2. Preheat the butter in a pan
  3. Fry the unions
  4. Add the sugar and the water
  5. Stir until the water has evaporated

Preparing the burger

  1. Preheat the barbeque, preferably to 250℃
  2. Shape the 200 gram meat into a burger by using a hamburger press
  3. Season the meat by adding some flake salt and pepper on both sides
  4. Place the meat and bacon on the grill
  5. When the bacon is done, flipp the burger, add the cheddar cheese and place the bacon on top of the burger
  6. Roast the brioche buns slightly on the grill. Once the buns are done, the burger is ready to be served!

How to serve

Apply one spoon of truffle mayonnaise onto the botten bun half, add some salad and apply the meat with the already applied cheddar cheese and bacon. Finally, place the caramelized unions and the top bun half.

Preferably, serve the burger with sweet potate fryes and a cold IPA beer.
